Have a look at some neat features of yapam

Sharing

With yapam you can share your secrets with your (colleagues|friends|neighbors) very easily, simply assign them to your secrets.
Under the hood yapam uses theirs and your public key to encrypt the secret and assigns the corresponding users to the secret. For more information about encryption head over to security.

Fileupload

In case you want to attach some documents to your secret, for example recovery codes for the login, simply attach them to your secret. After the upload completed yapam takes care of storing the document.
As possible storage engines yapam currently only supports the local filesystem. Fortunately additional storage engines are easily implemented.

Tags

This feature is currently under development, but it will allow you to tag your secrets. This makes it more easy to find your secret, as there is no need anymore for traversing a long list of directories.
