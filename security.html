Find out why only you can read your secrets

Encryption

yapam uses asymmetric keys to encrypt and decrypt your secrets.
Thats it!


You can find a deep dive into asymmetric here, but in short it means that you have two keys: a public one for encrypting and a private one for decrypting.

openPGP makes use of those two keys and even allows to use multiple public keys to encrypt a secret so multiple private keys can decrypt the data (sharing a secret).

Authorization
